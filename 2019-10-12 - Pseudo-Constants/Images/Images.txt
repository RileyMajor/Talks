Day 187: Tilt-Shift Miniature Fake 
Today, I went for a walk in the woods. The sun was streaming through the trees. The pine needles glowed orange and every leaf on every plant glowed green. A bird called somewhere in the distance, but other than that I was in utter solitude, and I reveled in it.
 
"Hold still!" A breathless voice interrupted the relative silence. I would have been scared, but when I heard the "click click" of a camera shutter, I knew who it was and I relaxed. Apparently, Boppy had gone into the woods in search of photographic inspiration, and when he saw me, he found it!
 
The result, after a bit of Photoshop, was this: a Tilt-Shift Miniature Fake. He explained that it is an effect often used to make a subject look like a toy.
 
"But Boppy--" I interjected, confused, "I am a toy!"
 
Either he didn't hear me, or he just didn't think it necessary to answer.
